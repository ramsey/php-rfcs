===== Introduction =====
The elevator pitch for the RFC. The first paragraph in this section will be slightly larger to give it emphasis; please write a good introduction.

===== Proposal =====
Ditch the current mailing list infrastructure (including  the following pieces on news.php.net: ecelerity, ezmlm, colubus serving news://news.php.net and the http://news.php.net website itself) and moving the current mailing lists (including the archives) to Google Groups.
